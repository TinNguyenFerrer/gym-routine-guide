# IMAGE RULES

Tieu chi nay dung de chon anh cho toan bo trang tap tai nha.

## Muc tieu chinh

- Uu tien anatomy/giai phau dang sach, dep, nen sang.
- Than nguoi xam/trang, nhom co tac dong to mau do/cam ro.
- Nhin hinh la phai thay ngay bai nay an vao nhom co nao.
- Moi card anh nen co thong tin ro: co chinh, co ho tro, va neu can thi co note fallback ngan.

## Thu tu uu tien khi chon anh

1. Local anatomy GIF trong `assets/exercises/`.
2. Local anatomy frame trong `assets/photos/`.
3. Poster anatomy/focus map neu chua co asset dung bien the.
4. Anh that chi la phuong an cuoi cung, va chi dung khi khong con cach nao khac.

## Dung cu duoc phep

- Xa don.
- Day khang luc.

## Dung cu khong duoc dung

- Ta don.
- Ta don kep.
- Thanh don.
- May tap.
- Bat ky dung cu nao khong phai tap tai nha voi xa va day khang luc.

## Rule cho moi bai tap

- Uu tien anatomy GIF loop neu da co san, vi GIF da the hien duoc nhieu pha dong tac.
- Neu khong co GIF loop, dung 2 frame anatomy:
  - Pha dau.
  - Pha cuoi.
- Neu van chua co asset dung bien the, dung poster anatomy thong tin, khong duoc chen anh sai dung cu chi de cho co hinh.

## Style anh uu tien

- Nen trang hoan toan hoac rat sang.
- Than nguoi ve xam, co an mau do.
- Khong co background phong gym, khong co vat the phu roi mat.
- Khong dung hinh co ta don, ta kep, may tap neu bai khong cho phep.
- Uu tien full body hoac it nhat du nhin duoc huong dong tac.
- Toan bo trang phai giu mot he style anatomy thong nhat, khong trộn style linh tinh.

## Chu thich tren giao dien

- Tren block anh phai uu tien:
  - Loai anh: anatomy loop / 2 pha / poster fallback.
  - Co chinh.
  - Co ho tro.
- Ben duoi card co the lap lai thong tin co chinh/co ho tro va cue nhanh.
- Chu thich ngan, de quet mat nhanh.

## Cach xu ly khi thieu anh

- Khong co local anatomy GIF:
  - Tim local anatomy frame.
- Khong co local anatomy frame:
  - Dung poster anatomy.
- Chi neu khong con lua chon nao moi dung anh that.
- Khong duoc doi sang anh sai dung cu chi vi dep hon.

## Tieu chi loai bo

- Loai anh bi roi, co nhieu doi tuong.
- Loai anh co nhieu mau sac khac style chung.
- Loai anh co tu the khong ro.
- Loai anh lam nguoi xem phai doc nhieu chu moi hieu dong tac.
- Loai anh sai dung cu so voi bai tap.
- Loai anh dep nhung khong noi ro nhom co tac dong.

## Ghi nho

- `assets/exercises/` la nguon uu tien so 1 neu co anatomy GIF dung bai.
- `assets/photos/` chi dung khi can frame anatomy theo pha.
- Neu phai chon giua dep va dung rule, uu tien dung rule va de hieu.
