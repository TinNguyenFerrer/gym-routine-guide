khong can gan thu.

Muc tieu: uu tien vai rong, lung mo, nguc day, chan van can doi. Lich nay chia theo Buoi 1 -> 2 -> 3 -> 4, nen neu ban bo lo 1 hom thi lan sau chi viec tap tiep buoi ke. Khong can dan lai theo thu 2, 3, 4.

Buoi 1 Buoi 2 Buoi 3 Buoi 4

Cach chay lich

  • Tuan dep thi tap 4 buoi: 1 -> 2 -> 3 -> 4.
  • Tuan ban chi tap 2-3 buoi: dung o dau thi lan sau tap tiep o do.
  • Moi buoi co muc tieu ~30 phut, uu tien form sach truoc khi them reps.

Rule de len co

  • Nghi 45-60 giay, rieng pull-up/chin-up co the nghi 75 giay.
  • Luon de lai 1-2 reps tru truoc khi fail, dung dot set 1 qua suc.
  • Khi dat muc reps cao nhat o tat ca cac set 2 vong lien tiep, tang do cang band hoac giam tempo 3 giay pha ha xuong.

Thay vi gan co dinh vao thu trong tuan, toan bo giao an duoc thiet ke theo vong lap. Ban chi can nho thu tu cac buoi. Neu tuan nay tap duoc 2 buoi thi dung o Buoi 2; lan tap tiep theo cua ban se la Buoi 3. Nhu vay lich khong bi vo, ma tong tan suat van deu cho nhung nhom co quan trong.

Khoi dong 3-5 phut truoc moi buoi
  • 20 vong xoay vai + 10 scap pulls/treo tha vai neu co xa.
  • 10 air squat + 10 hip hinge khong ta.
  • 1 set ramp-up nhe cho bai dau tien.
